Xbox Controller Diagram

  • A, B, X, Y Buttons: Action buttons
  • D-Pad: Directional pad for navigation
  • Left Stick: Left analog stick for movement
  • View, Menu Buttons: Additional buttons for menu navigation
  • Right Stick: Right analog stick for camera control
  • LB, RB: Left and right bumpers
  • LT, RT: Left and right triggers
  • Xbox Button: Guide button for accessing the Xbox menu
